Here is the description of the hotel

Hotel details This upscale hotel set among gardens is an 8-minute walk from Cubbon Park and 4 km from Bengaluru City Junction Railway Station. Elegant rooms feature free Wi-Fi and flat-screens, plus tea and coffeemaking equipment; some add balconies. Suites offer butler service, and sitting areas or living rooms. Club level quarters add access to a private lounge serving cocktails and snacks. Room service is available 24/7. Breakfast is complimentary. There are 5 restaurants, including a renowned Japanese eatery. Other amenities include a sleek whisky bar, a gym and a spa, plus an outdoor pool, a helipad, and a lobby with vertical gardens.
When was ITC Gardenia built?

Could a building in a tight space provide a solid transparency at the entrance and yet make you think it melts into the sky? The most talked about architectural construct in 2009 has been ITC's Gardenia in Bangalore.
